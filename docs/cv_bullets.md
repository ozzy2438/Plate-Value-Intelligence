# CV Bullets

## Confidential Freelance Data Science Contract

**Portfolio Pricing and Valuation Analytics | Confidential marketplace / asset-pricing client | NDA**

- Liaised with commercial, pricing and product stakeholders to translate portfolio valuation and premium-asset review requirements into a reproducible decision-support framework.
- Built an explainable valuation and portfolio-routing system from 17,782 governed auction records, using event-based validation and an untouched final holdout to reduce leakage risk.
- Developed Ridge, Random Forest and Gradient Boosting valuation models, achieving approximately GBP 1.25K final-test MAE and GBP 621 median absolute error while capturing 55% of top-decile premium assets.
- Designed a transparent premium and collectibility framework identifying 507 collectible-potential assets and routing approximately 10% of the portfolio to specialist human review rather than automated pricing.
- Converted valuation and rarity signals into fixed-price, standard-auction, premium-showcase and specialist-review recommendations for 1,981 final-event assets, supported by governed reserve-policy and sensitivity simulations.
